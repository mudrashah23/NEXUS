Welcome to our gym! We offer a comprehensive workout plan designed to help you build strength and achieve your fitness goals. Below is our weekly workout schedule:

Each day focuses on different muscle groups to ensure a balanced workout routine. Remember to warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterward.

Monday - Chest Day

Focus on building strength and mass in your chest muscles.

  1. Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  4. Push-ups: 3 sets to failure

Tuesday - Back Day

Target your back muscles for a strong and balanced physique.

  1. Pull-ups: 4 sets to failure
  2. Barbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  4. Dumbbell Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Wednesday - Shoulder Day

Build strong shoulders for better overall upper body strength.

  1. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Front Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  4. Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Thursday - Biceps Day

Focus on building bicep strength and size.

  1. Bicep Curls: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Concentration Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  4. Zottman Curls: 3 sets of 15-20

Friday - Leg Day

Strengthen your lower body with a comprehensive leg workout.

  1. Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Lunges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
  4. Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Saturday - Triceps + Cardio/Abs

Finish the week strong with triceps and cardio/abs.

Triceps Exercises

  1. Tricep Dips: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Skull Crushers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  4. Cardio (Running, Cycling, etc.): 20-30 minutes
  5. Planks: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Cardio

  1. Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
  2. Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Abs Exercises

  1. Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  2. Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  3. Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
